    Tutorial

    How to Get Your Instagram JSON Files

    Follow these steps to export your followers & following data directly from Meta Accounts Center.

    Safe Process

    Official Meta Export

    1. 1
      Go to Meta Accounts Center › Your information and permissions and select "Export your information".
    2. 2
      Click the "Create export" button to start a new request.
    3. 3
      Select the Instagram profile you want to analyze.
    4. 4
      Choose "Export to device" as your destination.
    5. 5
      Configure format:
      Customize information Followers & following
      Date Range All time
      Format JSON ✓
    6. 6
      Enter your password to confirm. Meta will begin processing your data.
    7. 7
      Wait for Meta to finish. You'll receive an email notification, or you can refresh the page until a Download button appears.
    8. 8
      Download the ZIP file and upload it directly using Quick Import. If needed, you can also extract it and upload followers_1.json and following.json manually.

    Export may take time

    Meta can take minutes or hours depending on account size.

    Use JSON format

    Choosing HTML format will not work with this analyzer.
